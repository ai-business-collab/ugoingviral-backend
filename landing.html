Frequently Asked Questions

How does UgoingViral work? +
You connect your social media accounts, and our AI generates content, schedules posts, and grows your audience automatically. No technical knowledge required — just connect and let it run.
Which platforms do you support? +
Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X (Twitter), and Facebook — with more platforms coming soon. Connect as many as your plan allows and post everywhere from one dashboard.
Can I try it for free? +
Yes — the free plan includes 3 AI-generated videos and 50 credits to get started. No payment required. You can upgrade anytime when you're ready to scale.
How do credits work? +
Credits are used for AI content generation — captions, hooks, video scripts, hashtags, and more. Each action costs a small amount of credits. Your plan includes a monthly credit allowance, and you can top up anytime from the billing page.
Is my content safe? +
Yes — your content and account data are stored securely and are never shared with third parties. We do not sell your data. Your social media credentials are stored encrypted and only used to post on your behalf.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — you can cancel your subscription anytime directly from your billing settings. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. Your account moves to the free plan and your content history is preserved.
