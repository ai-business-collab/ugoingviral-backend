Q & A

Frequently asked questions

What are credits and how are they used? +
Credits are the currency you use to generate AI content on the platform. Each script costs 5 credits, an AI video costs 20-80 credits depending on length, and a voiceover costs 15 credits. You receive new credits each month with your subscription.
Can I try the platform for free? +
Yes — create a free account and get 3 free AI videos. Test the platform before committing. Upgrade when you're ready. No credit card required.
Which platforms are supported? +
We support Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook and X (Twitter). You can post to multiple platforms at once and manage everything from one dashboard.
Are my accounts safe? +
We use official OAuth integrations for all platforms. Your passwords are never stored — we only store the access tokens the platforms provide us. Our anti-ban system ensures natural activity that does not violate platform guidelines.
Can I cancel at any time? +
Yes, no commitment. You can cancel your subscription at any time and keep access for the rest of the month. No hidden fees.
What happens if I run out of credits? +
You can buy extra credits at any time: 100 credits for 9, 500 credits for 9 or 1,500 credits for 49. You can also upgrade your plan to get more monthly credits.
