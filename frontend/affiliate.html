Questions

Frequently asked

When and how do I get paid? +

Commissions are processed monthly. We pay via bank transfer or PayPal. Minimum payout threshold is $50. Payments are processed within the first week of each month for the previous month's earnings.

How long does the cookie last? +

Our referral tracking uses a 90-day cookie. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 90 days, you'll receive commission on all their payments — forever, as long as they remain a customer.

Is there a minimum audience size? +

No minimum. Whether you have 500 Twitter followers or 500,000 YouTube subscribers, you're welcome to apply. We care more about relevance and quality than raw numbers.

Can I use paid ads to promote my link? +

You may use paid social media ads, but you may not bid on "UgoingViral" branded keywords in search ads. All ad creatives must be approved by us before going live.

What if a referred user cancels and comes back? +

If a user cancels and re-subscribes later using your affiliate link, you'll earn commission on their new subscription. If they come back without using your link, it depends on whether the 90-day cookie is still active.
